Blue-Ringed Octopus

The blue-ringed octopus (Hapalochlaena), known for its namesake azure spots. But though beautiful, those blue rings signify danger.

  • Scientific Name: Hapalochlaena maculosa
  • Average length: 10 centimetres
  • Average Lifespan: 2 years
  • Habitat: Sea of Southern Australia
